When I was a junior in college I read Glimmer by Warren Berger which heavily featured the work of Bruce Mau. I was immediately inspired by his philosophy and especially by his Incomplete Manifesto For Growth. In my studio class I used his manifesto as the text for a loose leaf book featuring illustrations inspired by several of the bullet points in his manifesto.
